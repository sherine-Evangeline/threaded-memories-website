About Threaded Memories

How It All Began

A Passion Stitched in Time

Threaded Memories was born from a simple belief — that the most meaningful gifts are the ones made by hand. What started as a hobby in a small room in Chennai has blossomed into a beloved embroidery studio trusted by hundreds of clients across India.

Our founder discovered the art of embroidery from her grandmother, who believed every stitch should carry intention and love. That philosophy lives on in every piece we create today — from delicate monograms to intricate floral portraits.

We don't just embroider fabric — we embroider emotions, memories, and milestones into keepsakes that last generations.
