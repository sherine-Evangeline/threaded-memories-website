Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a custom order take?

Depending on the complexity, most orders are completed within 5–14 business days. Rush orders can be accomodated for an additional fee.

Do you ship across India?

Yes! We ship nationwide with secure packaging. International shipping is also available upon request.

Can I provide my own fabric?

Absolutely. You can send us your own fabric, or we can source premium materials for you from our curated collection.

What if I'm not sure about the design?

No worries! We offer a free consultation to help you brainstorm and finalize the perfect design before any work begins.
